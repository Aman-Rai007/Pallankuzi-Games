📜 How to Play Pallankuzhi

  • Goal: Capture more seeds than your opponent.
  • Sowing: Click a pit on your side. Seeds drop one-by-one counter-clockwise.
  • Continuing: If the last seed lands next to a pit WITH seeds, pick those up and continue.
  • Capturing: If the last seed lands next to an EMPTY pit, capture all seeds from the pit right after it!
  • Game Over: When one player has no seeds left on their side.
